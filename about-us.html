About Village Cup

Village Cup is a family-owned business that was established during the height of the pandemic. Despite the challenges, the De Jesus family remained resilient and started their venture from the comfort of their own home. With their passion for good food and drinks, they started creating a menu of premium beverages like milk tea and coffee, and delicious snacks such as burgers, fries, quesadillas, takoyaki, and more. Their dedication to quality and affordability quickly gained the attention of the community. The De Jesus family takes pride in serving their customers with the best possible service, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel at home.
